Procedural Thinking

Part of the long-term memory that is responsible for knowing who to do things. Often referred to as Motor Skills, it's the memories stored information on how to do things. Super Procedural Thinkers have a superior ability to form procedural memories and are very logical in their thinking which can assist becoming a better Programmer

Computers Are Stupid

I remember from my initial programming days GIGO or Garbage In Garbage Out . Basically a Computer (CPU) is going to do exactly what you tell it to do. Coding is very exact almost like a Science. If you miss one character or letter the CPU may not understand the command hence display Garbage as the output.
